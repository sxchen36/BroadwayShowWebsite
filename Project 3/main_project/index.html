On 19 December 2013

Your Turn, Heather! Tickets Now Available for the Off-Broadway Premiere of Heathers the Musical
Tickets are now on sale for Heathers the Musical. The musical adaptation of the cult film features a book, music and lyrics by Tony nominee Laurence O'Keefe (Legally Blonde) and Emmy winner Kevin Murphy (Reefer Madness). Heathers the Musical will begin performances March 17 at New World World Stages, with opening night is set for March 31.

Conor McPherson's The Night Alive, Starring Ciaran Hinds & Jim Norton, Extends Off-Broadway
This Night isn't coming to an end just yet! Atlantic Theater Company's American premiere presentation of Conor McPherson's The Night Alive has extended its off-Broadway run at the Linda Gross Theatre through February 2, 2014.
